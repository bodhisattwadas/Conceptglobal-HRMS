Attendance and Leaves
Real-time attendance processing with multiple options for capturing attendance and biometric hardware integration. Open HRMS attendance tracking system is integrated with payroll and leave management system; delivering you all the intuitive features needed for an efficient attendance management system.

Multiple Attendance Capturing Methods
The attendance of employees can be captured from multiple sources.
> Employee login portal where he/she can mark his attendance.
> Third-party hardware like Biometric devices.
> Kiosk mode, from which the employee can mark his attendance using their PIN or swiping the ID card.

Biometric Device Configuration
Integrate thumbing or face-detection biometric devices, ensure flawless and seamless attendance capturing.

Attendance Regularization
Manage attendances of employees who have gone for onsite work or any company need. A regularization request can be created, and the attendance will be added to the registry on approving it.

Leave Policy
Configure different types of leaves like sick leave, compensatory leave, unpaid leave, etc., and create a transparent leave policy for your company.

Automatic Leave Request
Configure a leave request email, and every email in the desired format will automatically get converted to a leave request in Open HRMS.

Leave Approval
Set multiple approval points for particular leaves so that concerned authorities can effectively review the leave requests.
Manage Vacation
Receive employee vacation requests and handle them more organized. Get the leave notification and sanction vacation with/without salary. View the overlapping leave requests and manage them appropriately, assign the pending tasks to others, and more with Open HRMS.
